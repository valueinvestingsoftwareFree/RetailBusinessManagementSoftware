Lecture 13. Generation of a Graphic Catalog of Items

In this lecture you will learn how to generate a graphic catalog of items inside the system.

A graphic catalog allows products to be displayed visually with images, descriptions, and organized categories, improving usability and product browsing experience.

You will learn how to structure item images, assign visual references to products, and generate a clean catalog layout for better presentation and navigation.

The lecture also explains how the graphic catalog integrates with inventory, sales modules, and product management workflows inside the software.

This feature is especially useful for retail environments where visual identification of products improves speed, accuracy, and customer interaction.

The Udemy course includes a step-by-step practical demonstration showing how to generate and manage a complete graphical product catalog.
