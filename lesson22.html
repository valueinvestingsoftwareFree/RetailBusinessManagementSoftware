Lecture 22. Record of Purchase Invoice Payments

In this lecture you will learn how to record purchase invoice payments inside the system.

Purchase payments are essential for keeping supplier accounts up to date and ensuring accurate financial control over outstanding invoices.

You will learn how to register payments against purchase invoices, select payment methods, and manage partial or full payments depending on business needs.

The lecture also explains how payment records affect accounts payable, supplier balances, and overall financial reporting within the system.

Proper payment tracking ensures accurate accounting, better cash flow control, and improved supplier relationship management.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to record purchase invoice payments using real business scenarios inside the application.
