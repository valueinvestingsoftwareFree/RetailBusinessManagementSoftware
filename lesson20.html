Lecture 20. Record Supplier Credit Notes

In this lecture you will learn how to record supplier credit notes inside the system.

Supplier credit notes are used to handle returns, purchase adjustments, pricing corrections, or billing discrepancies with suppliers.

You will learn how to create supplier credit notes, link them to purchase records, select suppliers, and define accurate adjustment values.

The lecture also explains how supplier credit notes affect accounts payable, inventory adjustments, and financial reporting within the system.

Proper management of supplier credit notes ensures accurate inventory control, correct financial records, and better supplier relationship management.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to record supplier credit notes using real-world examples inside the application.
