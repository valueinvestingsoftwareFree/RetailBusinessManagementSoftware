Lecture 14. Create Zones and Assign Customers to Zones

In this lecture you will learn how to create zones and assign customers to them inside the system.

Zones help organize customers geographically or logically, making it easier to manage sales regions, delivery areas, and operational planning.

You will learn how to define different zones, structure them according to business needs, and assign each customer to a specific zone for better control and segmentation.

The lecture also explains how zones can improve reporting, route optimization, and customer analysis within the software system.

This feature is especially useful for businesses that handle multiple regions, field sales teams, or delivery logistics operations.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to create zones and manage customer assignments effectively.
