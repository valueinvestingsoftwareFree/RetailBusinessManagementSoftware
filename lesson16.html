Lecture 16. Record Sales

In this lecture you will learn how to record sales transactions inside the system.

Sales recording is a core process that updates inventory, tracks revenue, and maintains accurate customer purchase history.

You will learn how to create sales documents, select customers, assign salespeople, add products, define quantities, and manage pricing details.

The lecture also explains how sales automatically affect inventory levels, financial records, and reporting modules within the system.

Proper sales management ensures accurate business control, improves decision-making, and maintains consistent operational data across all modules.

The Udemy course includes a step-by-step practical demonstration showing the full sales workflow using real-world scenarios inside the application.
