Lecture 25. Record of Advance Payments to Customers

In this lecture you will learn how to record advance payments from customers inside the system.

Advance customer payments occur when clients pay before receiving goods or services, helping businesses secure cash flow and confirm future sales.

You will learn how to register advance payments, assign them to customers, select payment methods, and link them to future sales invoices.

The lecture also explains how advance payments affect customer balances, accounts receivable, and invoice settlement processes within the system.

Proper handling of advance payments ensures accurate financial tracking, improved cash management, and better customer transaction control.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to record customer advance payments using real business scenarios inside the application.
