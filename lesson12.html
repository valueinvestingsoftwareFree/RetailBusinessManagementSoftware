Lecture 12. Update Item Prices

In this lecture you will learn how to update item prices inside the system.

Price updates are essential for maintaining accurate sales operations, adapting to market changes, and ensuring proper profitability management.

You will learn how to modify product prices manually, apply pricing adjustments to existing inventory items, and manage different price levels efficiently.

The lecture also explains how updated prices integrate with price lists, customer sales operations, quotations, and inventory transactions throughout the software.

Proper price management helps businesses maintain organized commercial strategies, improve operational consistency, and respond quickly to supplier or market price changes.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to update item prices correctly using real workflow examples inside the application.
