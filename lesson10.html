Lecture 10. Create Price Lists

In this lecture you will learn how to create and manage Price Lists inside the system.

Price lists allow businesses to organize product prices efficiently for different customers, sales strategies, regions, distributors, or business scenarios.

You will learn how to define price structures, assign prices to products, and configure multiple pricing levels according to your operational needs.

The lecture also explains how price lists can simplify sales processes and improve pricing consistency across inventory operations.

Proper price list management helps businesses maintain organized commercial strategies, improve customer service, and optimize sales workflows.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to create, configure, and manage price lists using real examples inside the software.
