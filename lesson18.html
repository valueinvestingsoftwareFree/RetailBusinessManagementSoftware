Lecture 18. Inventory Kardex: Tracking Balance, Entries, Exits, Costs & Profits

In this lecture you will learn how to use the Inventory Kardex to track stock movement inside the system.

The Kardex is a detailed record that shows every inventory movement, including entries, exits, balances, costs, and profit calculations over time.

You will understand how purchases increase stock, sales decrease stock, and how the system maintains a real-time and historical view of product quantities.

The lecture also explains how costs and profits are calculated based on inventory movements, helping businesses evaluate product performance accurately.

This tool is essential for maintaining control over stock levels, identifying discrepancies, and improving financial decision-making.

The Udemy course includes a practical walkthrough showing how to analyze the Kardex step by step using real business scenarios.
