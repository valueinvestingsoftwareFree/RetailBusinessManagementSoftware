Lecture 4. Designing an Inventory Hierarchy: Classes, Categories & Products

In this lecture you will learn how to design a structured inventory hierarchy using Classes, Categories, and Products.

A well-organized inventory structure helps businesses classify products efficiently, improve reporting accuracy, and simplify inventory management operations.

You will understand how classes can group major business areas, how categories help organize related items, and how products are assigned within the hierarchy for better control and scalability.

This structure becomes especially useful when managing large inventories with hundreds or thousands of products across different departments or business units.

The Udemy course provides a detailed practical explanation with real examples to help you design a clean and professional inventory system from the beginning.
