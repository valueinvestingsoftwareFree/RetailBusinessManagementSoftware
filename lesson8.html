Lecture 8. Record Purchases

In this lecture you will learn how to record Purchases inside the system.

Purchase transactions are essential for updating inventory quantities, tracking supplier operations, and maintaining accurate stock records.

You will learn how to create purchase documents, select suppliers, add products, define quantities, enter prices, and manage transaction details efficiently.

The lecture also explains how purchase records automatically affect inventory balances and become part of the business transaction history.

Proper purchase registration improves inventory control, purchasing analysis, supplier management, and reporting accuracy across the entire system.

The Udemy course includes a complete practical demonstration showing the full purchase workflow step by step using real examples.
