Lecture 24. Record of Sale Invoice Payments

In this lecture you will learn how to record sale invoice payments inside the system.

Sales payments are essential for tracking customer collections, updating accounts receivable, and maintaining accurate financial records.

You will learn how to register payments against sales invoices, select payment methods, and handle full or partial payments based on customer transactions.

The lecture also explains how payment records affect customer balances, cash flow, and financial reporting within the system.

Proper payment management ensures accurate accounting, improved collection control, and better overall business financial stability.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to record sales invoice payments using real business scenarios inside the application.
