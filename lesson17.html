Lecture 17. Modify or Delete Items in a Sale

In this lecture you will learn how to modify or delete items within a sales transaction.

During real business operations, sales records often need adjustments due to quantity corrections, pricing changes, customer requests, or data entry errors.

You will learn how to safely edit sales items while maintaining accurate inventory updates, financial consistency, and transaction integrity.

The lecture also explains how removing items from a sale affects stock levels, totals, and related reports within the system.

Proper handling of sales modifications ensures reliable data, accurate reporting, and better operational control across the entire business workflow.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to update and delete sale items correctly using real examples.
