Lecture 11. Creating Customers

In this lecture you will learn how to create and manage Customers inside the system.

Customer records are essential for sales management, invoicing processes, transaction history tracking, and business relationship organization.

You will learn how to register customer information such as names, contact details, addresses, phone numbers, email information, and geographic map locations.

The lecture also explains how customer records integrate with sales operations, price lists, reporting modules, and inventory transactions throughout the software.

Proper customer organization helps businesses improve operational efficiency, customer tracking, and long-term commercial management.

The Udemy course includes a detailed practical walkthrough demonstrating how to create customer records step by step using real examples inside the application.
