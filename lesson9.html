Lecture 9. Modify or Delete Items in a Purchase

In this lecture you will learn how to modify or delete items within a purchase transaction.

During real business operations, purchase records may require adjustments because of quantity corrections, pricing changes, supplier updates, or data entry mistakes.

You will learn how to edit existing purchase items safely while maintaining accurate inventory balances and transaction consistency.

The lecture also explains how to remove products from a purchase document and how those changes automatically affect stock quantities and related calculations inside the system.

Proper management of purchase modifications helps maintain reliable inventory records and improves operational accuracy across purchasing processes.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to update and delete purchase items correctly using real workflow examples.
