Future Development Roadmap

1. Sales Forecasting Engine

Forecast future sales using historical trends, seasonality, and business growth patterns.

2. Inventory Replenishment Planner

Estimate optimal purchase quantities based on historical demand and inventory turnover.

3. Dead Inventory Detection

Identify products that have not sold for extended periods and may be tying up capital.

4. Fast-Moving Product Analysis

Discover the products generating the highest sales velocity.

5. Customer Profitability Analysis

Measure which customers contribute the most revenue and profit to the business.

6. Supplier Performance Scorecard

Evaluate suppliers based on pricing, reliability, delivery times, and purchase volume.

7. Product Profitability Ranking

Rank products according to gross profit contribution and overall profitability.

8. Salesperson Performance Dashboard

Compare sales activity, revenue generation, collections, and customer growth.

9. Route Profitability Analysis

Analyze the profitability of sales territories and customer routes.

10. Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis

Identify purchasing patterns and customer buying habits over time.

11. Inventory Aging Dashboard

Monitor how long inventory remains in stock before being sold.

12. Automatic Purchase Suggestions

Generate intelligent recommendations for inventory purchases based on stock levels and sales history.

13. Business Health Score

Measure the overall operational health of the business using inventory, sales, profit, and cash indicators.

14. Cash Flow Projection

Estimate future cash inflows and outflows using sales, purchases, and outstanding balances.

15. Scenario Planning Simulator

Evaluate the financial impact of potential business decisions before implementing them.
