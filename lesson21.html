Lecture 21. Create Payment Methods

In this lecture you will learn how to create and configure payment methods inside the system.

Payment methods define how customers and suppliers can complete transactions, such as cash, bank transfers, credit, or other customized payment options.

You will learn how to register new payment methods, define their behavior, and integrate them into sales and purchase processes.

The lecture also explains how payment methods affect transaction tracking, financial reporting, and accounting consistency within the system.

Proper configuration of payment methods ensures flexible payment handling, better control of cash flow, and improved business operations.

The Udemy course includes a practical step-by-step demonstration showing how to create and manage payment methods inside real business scenarios.
