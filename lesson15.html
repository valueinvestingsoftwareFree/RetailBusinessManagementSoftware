Lecture 15. Create Salespeople

In this lecture you will learn how to create and manage salespeople inside the system.

Salespeople are essential for tracking sales performance, assigning customers, and organizing commercial activities within the business.

You will learn how to register salesperson profiles including personal information, contact details, assigned zones, and commission structures if applicable.

The lecture also explains how salespeople interact with customers, sales records, and reporting tools within the system.

Proper salesperson management helps improve sales tracking, accountability, and performance analysis across the organization.

The Udemy course includes a complete practical demonstration showing how to create and manage salespeople step by step in real scenarios.
