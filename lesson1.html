Lecture 1. Get the Software for This Course - Free Download and Installation Guide

Welcome to the course. In this first lecture you will learn how to obtain the free business accounting, inventory, sales, purchases, and stock management software used throughout the entire training program.

The software has been developed continuously for years with the objective of helping small businesses, entrepreneurs, stores, distributors, and independent professionals manage their operations more efficiently without expensive subscriptions.

What Is Included in the Software

The application used in this course combines multiple business management tools inside a single ecosystem. Students will learn how to manage accounting operations, inventory, sales, purchases, customer balances, supplier accounts, expenses, stock movement, and financial analysis using practical real-world workflows.

Unlike many commercial systems that require monthly payments, this project focuses on long-term accessibility, offline functionality, free support, and continuous improvement driven by real business needs and user feedback.

The course includes demonstrations using both desktop and mobile components of the software ecosystem, helping students understand how modern business management can be simplified through practical tools and organized workflows.

Features Covered Throughout the Course

  • Expense tracking and budgeting
  • Customer account management
